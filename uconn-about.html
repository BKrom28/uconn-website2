5 Fun Facts about Uconn Stamford

-In 1952 Uconn Stamford was founded as a regional campus.

-In the mid 1970's Uconn Stamford started to offer 4 year degrees

-The new campus opened in 1998.

-Uconn Stamford now has over 115 majors.

-This year was Uconn's biggest freshman class of all time

5 Fun Facts about Uconn Basketball

-Uconn is the only school to have both basketball teams win D1 championships in the same year.

-The Womens basketball program holds the record for most consectutive wins 90.

-The Uconn men have won every time they have gotten to the NCAA Final.

-Before becoming the Huskies in 1933, the university's mascot was the Aggies.

-All of the mascots are named Jonathan after Jonathan Trumbull, former governor of Connecticut.
